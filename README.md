# Upgrade to Epic (HoH2 Mod)

**Upgrade to Epic** is a mod for *Heroes of Hammerwatch II* that allows players to upgrade their **Rare** quality equipment to **Epic** quality at the Enchanter shop in town.

---

## Features

- **Epic Quality Upgrades**: Adds a new tab/option at the Enchanter in town to upgrade Rare items directly to Epic status.
- **Enchanting Progression**: Allows players to scale up and maximize their gear potential by transitioning Rare items to Epic quality.
- **Resource Spending**: Upgrades require appropriate materials (e.g. rare/epic essence) and gold.

---

## How to Install (Local)

1. Copy the `upgrade_to_epic` folder into your game's `mods/` directory:
   `...\SteamLibrary\steamapps\common\Heroes of Hammerwatch 2\mods\upgrade_to_epic\`
2. Launch the game, open the **Edit Mods** menu on your profile, and enable **Upgrade to Epic**.

---

## How to Use

1. Enter the town and visit the **Enchanter**.
2. Interact with the enchanter and open the **Equipment Upgrade/Enchant** tab.
3. Select your Rare equipped item and pay the required gold and materials to upgrade it to Epic quality.

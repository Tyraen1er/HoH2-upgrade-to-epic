# Upgrade and Enchant Epic (HoH2 Mod)

**Upgrade and Enchant Epic** is a mod for *Heroes of Hammerwatch II* that allows players to upgrade their **Rare** quality equipment to **Epic** quality, as well as enchant the newly unlocked **3rd (Epic) enchantment slot** at the Enchanter shop in town.

---

## Features

- **Epic Quality Upgrades**: Adds an option at the Enchanter in town to upgrade Rare items directly to Epic quality.
- **3rd Enchantment Slot (Epic)**: Extends the Enchanter's interface with a 3rd slot (Epic) for eligible armor items (Head, Chest, Feet, Hands, Back) allowing you to apply powerful Epic enchantments.
- **Resource Balancing**: Balanced gold and material requirements (Crystals and Dust) to match game progression.

---

## How to Install (Local)

1. Copy the `upgrade_and_enchant_epic` folder into your game's `mods/` directory:
   `...\SteamLibrary\steamapps\common\Heroes of Hammerwatch 2\mods\upgrade_and_enchant_epic\`
2. Launch the game, open the **Edit Mods** menu on your profile, and enable **Upgrade and Enchant Epic**.

---

## How to Use

1. Enter the town and visit the **Enchanter**.
2. Interact with the enchanter and select your equipped gear.
3. You can upgrade **Rare** quality items to **Epic** quality by paying the required materials and gold.
4. Once an item is Epic quality, select it to customize and apply powerful Epic enchantments to the **3rd slot**.
